SPA Acts and Statute

SPA Act - 2014

As an Institute of national importance, the School of Planning and Architecture Vijayawada comes under the purview of an Act of the Parliament of India, which is "The School of Planning and Architecture (SPA) Act, 2014" to promote education and research in architectural study including the planning of human settlements.
